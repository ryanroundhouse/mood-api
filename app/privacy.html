How We Utilize Your Data

We handle your data with the utmost care and in accordance with the plan you have selected:

Free Plan

Your data is securely stored and presented back to you in a user-friendly format, allowing you to track and review your mood entries.

Pro Plan

Your data is anonymized to protect your privacy. We then utilize advanced analytical tools, including a Large Language Model (LLM), to provide you with deeper insights and personalized recommendations based on your mood data. Additionally, the Pro Plan allows you to export your data.
