Privacy Policy

Committed to Protecting Your Privacy

Information We Collect

To provide you with the best possible service, we collect the following essential information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Mood-Related Information
    • Mood Rating
    • Personal Reflections (Optional)
    • Associated Activities
    • Timestamp Information
    • Source of Entry (website, app, or email)
  • Sleep Data (Optional - via Garmin Connect Integration)
    • Sleep Duration
    • Sleep Quality Metrics
    • Sleep Stages
    • Sleep Timestamps

This information is collected for your personal use and insight. Sleep data is only collected if you choose to connect your Garmin device through our optional Garmin Connect integration. Access is strictly controlled and limited to authorized Moodful personnel who maintain and secure our systems.


Data Management Practices

Your trust is our priority. Here's how we protect your information:

Security Infrastructure

Your privacy and data security are paramount to us. We implement enterprise-grade encryption protocols for all stored data, including mood entries and analytical summaries. Our comprehensive security infrastructure ensures your personal information remains confidential and protected through multiple layers of security measures.

Should you wish to exercise your right to data deletion, please submit a formal request through our secure contact portal using your registered email address.

Secure Data Transfer

We employ state-of-the-art TLS (Transport Layer Security) encryption protocols for all data transmissions between your devices and our secure servers. This enterprise-level security measure ensures complete confidentiality of your personal information and mood data during transmission, protecting against any unauthorized access or interception attempts.

Service Tiers and Data Processing

We maintain rigorous data handling standards across our service tiers:

Basic Plan

Your data is maintained in our secure environment and presented through an intuitive interface for personal tracking and reflection. By default, all analytics processing occurs within our secure infrastructure, ensuring your information never leaves our protected environment.

Optional Enhanced AI Insights: You may choose to opt-in to enhanced AI-powered insights, which involves securely sharing your mood entry data with our trusted Large Language Model for advanced analysis. If you have connected your Garmin device through our optional Garmin Connect integration, your sleep data will also be shared with our secure LLM alongside your mood data to provide more comprehensive insights. This opt-in feature provides deeper personalized insights while maintaining strict security protocols. You can enable or disable this feature at any time through your account settings.

Professional Plan

Experience enhanced insights through our advanced analytics suite, powered by sophisticated AI technology including our secure Large Language Model (LLM). This premium service provides detailed pattern analysis and personalized insights while maintaining strict data privacy standards. Additionally, we offer secure data export capabilities for your convenience.
